Find nu partial M / partial T |H field H has units A/m mu0 = permeability of free space in H/m = T m /A

= 4. * np.pi * 1.e-7

mu_0 H = T m /A A/m = T

Bohr magneton in J/T beta = 5.050783699e-27

Magnetization is number density * Bohr magneton M has units (1/m^3) J/T (energy density per Telsa) Tesla T = J / A / m^2 Alternately: M ~ (1/m^3) J / T = A m^-1 nu is the specific volume, m^3 / g nu M has units A m^2 / g = J/T/g

mu dM/dt * mu0 H has units J/g/K, or entropy per gram

c0 has units T A^-1 m^-2 g Note: this is a typo in Gallagher, who gives: c0_gallagher ~ T A^-1 m^-1 g

J T^-1 m^-3 = J (A m^2 / J) m^-3 = A m^-1, A = J T^-1 m^-2 units are 1/units of c0, or T^-1 A m g = T^-1 J/T m^-2 m g
